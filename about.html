About Us

LegalAIPay was created by a legal professional with over 20 years of experience in privacy, data protection, and cross-border data law. Our founder is admitted to the New York and PRC (China) bars and has built a career primarily as in-house counsel, and is a member of the International Association of Privacy Professionals (IAPP).

We built LegalAIPay because small and mid-sized businesses face real privacy and data-protection obligations across the US, EU/UK, and China, but rarely have the budget for continuous legal support. Our tools turn that complexity into clear, structured next steps: DPIAs, breach response plans, DPAs, privacy notices, contract reviews, and cross-border transfer checks, so you walk into any compliance conversation already prepared.

Our tools run entirely in your browser. Your inputs stay on your device, and every workflow mirrors how a real assessment should be documented.

A note on scope

Our tools provide general information and AI-assisted drafts as a starting point. They are not legal advice, and using them does not create an attorney-client relationship. For decisions affecting contracts, filings, or fundamental rights, we recommend review by qualified counsel.
