About Us关于我们

LegalAIPay was created by a legal professional with over 20 years of experience in privacy, data protection, and cross-border data law. Our founder is admitted to the New York and PRC (China) bars and has built a career primarily as in-house counsel, and is a member of the International Association of Privacy Professionals (IAPP).LegalAIPay 由一位在隐私、数据保护和跨境数据法领域拥有 20 多年经验的法律专业人士创建。创始人同时拥有美国纽约州和中国的律师执业资格，主要以企业内部法务的身份积累职业经验，并且是国际隐私专业人士协会（IAPP）的会员。

We built LegalAIPay because small and mid-sized businesses face real privacy and data-protection obligations across the US, EU/UK, and China, but rarely have the budget for continuous legal support. Our tools turn that complexity into clear, structured next steps: DPIAs, breach response plans, DPAs, privacy notices, contract reviews, and cross-border transfer checks, so you walk into any compliance conversation already prepared.我们创建 LegalAIPay，是因为中小企业在美国、欧盟/英国和中国都面临实实在在的隐私与数据保护义务，却很少有预算获得持续的法律支持。我们的工具把这些复杂性转化为清晰、有条理的后续步骤：数据保护影响评估、数据泄露应对方案、数据处理协议、隐私告知、合同审查以及跨境传输核查，让您在任何合规沟通中都已做好准备。

Our tools run entirely in your browser. Your inputs stay on your device, and every workflow mirrors how a real assessment should be documented.我们的工具完全在您的浏览器中运行。您输入的内容只保留在您的设备上，每个流程都按照真实评估应有的记录方式来呈现。

A note on scope关于适用范围的说明

Our tools provide general information and AI-assisted drafts as a starting point. They are not legal advice, and using them does not create an attorney-client relationship. For decisions affecting contracts, filings, or fundamental rights, we recommend review by qualified counsel.我们的工具提供一般性信息和 AI 辅助草稿，仅作为起点。它们不构成法律意见，使用它们也不会形成律师-委托人关系。对于涉及合同、申报或基本权利的决定，我们建议由具备资质的律师审查。
