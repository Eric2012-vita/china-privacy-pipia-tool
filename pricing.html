FAQ

Positioning guardrails

Is this legal advice?

No. The product creates lawyer-reviewable drafts and evidence records. High-risk processing, filings, disputes, and material contracts still need counsel review.

Why pay if tools are free?

Users pay for continuity: export packets, version history, reminders, client workspaces, and a defensible audit trail.

Who is the first paid customer?

EU/US small businesses with SaaS, ecommerce, AI, HR, or cross-border vendor data flows. China capability is the differentiator.

What should be added next?

Live Stripe/Paddle links, webhook-issued signed export tokens, account workspaces, and email reminders.
