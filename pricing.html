How bundles work

No login. Your credits live in your browser.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need an account?

No. The free tools and single export require no account. Bundle credits are stored in your browser and can be restored with a recovery token on any device.

What if I clear my browser?

Save your recovery token shown on the thank-you page after payment. Paste it into any tool page to instantly restore your remaining bundle credits.

Which documents can I export?

Any document generated on legalaipay.com — Privacy Policy (China/EU/US), DPIA, PIPIA report, US state compliance report, and GDPR kit outputs.

Is this legal advice?

No. The tools create lawyer-reviewable drafts and compliance records. For high-risk processing, filings, or disputes, have qualified counsel review the output before reliance.

Why are the tools free to preview?

You invest time filling in your details first — so you can verify the output is correct before paying to export it. No surprises.

What payment methods are accepted?

PayPal, and any card PayPal supports (Visa, Mastercard, Amex). You do not need a PayPal account — PayPal's guest checkout accepts cards directly.
