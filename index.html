If I run a business, what do I actually need to do?

No legal background needed. Here is the path from "I have no idea if we are compliant" to "we are covered" — in seven plain steps. 没有法律背景也没关系，七个简单步骤带您从零到合规。

Map where your customers are

Browse all tools浏览全部工具
2
Check your privacy risk (free)
评估隐私风险（免费）
For China, run a PIPIA. For the EU/UK, run a DPIA. These guided assessments flag what could get you fined — before a regulator does.
3
Put a privacy policy in place
发布隐私政策
Generate a clear, jurisdiction-aware privacy policy so customers know how you handle their data and you meet the legal "tell people" requirement.
4
Get your contracts right
审查合同
When you share data with vendors or partners, you need the right clauses. Use the contract and data-agreement tools to add them and review what you sign.
5
Handle cross-border data safely
安全跨境传输数据
Sending data between the EU, China and the US? The transfer tool shows you the lawful path and the paperwork you need.
6
Be ready if something goes wrong
做好应急准备
If there is a data breach, the clock starts ticking (72 hours in the EU). The breach tool walks you through who to notify and when.
7
Keep it up to date
持续合规
Rules change and so does your business. Re-run the checks each year — China now expects a regular compliance audit, and the audit tool keeps the loop closed.
